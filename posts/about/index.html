About Me

Hello, welcome to my personal website. My name is Mahmudul Hasan. I am from Dhaka, BD.

In my personal website I will share my thoughts about my vision: ideas I think of, technologies I used or tried, my likes and dislikes about occurence on tech-world. You might agree, or disagree. Like or do not like. I expect you to write it down in comment sections, on DM me on twitter.

I have been doing software engineering by profession, and working actively from 2008 till today. I work in web-based software development in PHP, Node.js oriented technologies. However, I also write software on Python, Rust, etc. I have great enthusiasm for software system design, infrastructure, and system programming. I mostly work with backend technologies. You may also find some articles on these topics too. I will also share my codes on github, and link those here.
